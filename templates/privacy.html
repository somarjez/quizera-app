Introduction

Welcome to Quizera's Privacy Policy. This document establishes the framework for how Quizera ("we," "us," or "our") collects, processes, stores, and protects personal information from users ("you" or "your") of our educational assessment platform and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using Quizera, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any provision of this policy, you should discontinue use of our Services immediately.

Scope of Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Quizera platform, including students, educators, administrators, and institutional partners. It covers all data collection activities conducted through our website, mobile applications, and associated services.

2

Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when registering for an account, using our Services, or communicating with us:

  • Account Registration Data: Full name, email address, username, password, institutional affiliation, role (student/educator), and grade level or subject area
  • Profile Information: Optional biographical information, profile photographs, educational background, and academic interests
  • Educational Content: Quiz responses, assignments, test submissions, programming code, essays, and other academic work products
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our platform, support inquiries, feedback submissions, and correspondence with our team
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions, we collect billing details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access our Services, we automatically collect certain technical and usage information:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, and hardware specifications
  • Usage Analytics: Pages viewed, features accessed, time spent on platform, navigation patterns, quiz completion rates, and performance metrics
  • Location Data: General geographic location derived from IP address (city and country level only)
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Session identifiers, preference settings, and authentication tokens

2.3 Information from Educational Institutions

If you access Quizera through an educational institution, we may receive:

  • Student roster information (names, student IDs, email addresses, class enrollments)
  • Integration data from learning management systems (LMS)
  • Administrative records as authorized by the institution
Special Category Data

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, health data, or biometric information unless explicitly required for accessibility purposes and with your informed consent.

How We Use Your Information

Quizera processes your personal information for the following legitimate purposes:

3.1 Educational Service Delivery

  • Providing access to quiz creation, administration, and grading functionalities
  • Generating personalized learning recommendations and adaptive content
  • Tracking academic progress and performance analytics
  • Facilitating communication between students and educators
  • Enabling collaborative learning features and peer interactions

3.2 Platform Improvement and Research

  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience and interface design
  • Conducting educational research and developing pedagogical insights (using aggregated, de-identified data)
  • Testing new features and assessing platform performance
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues

3.3 Security and Compliance

  • Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and security breaches
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and academic integrity policies
  • Complying with legal obligations and responding to lawful requests from authorities
  • Protecting the rights, property, and safety of Quizera, our users, and the public

3.4 Communication and Support

  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support
  • Sending service-related notifications, updates, and security alerts
  • Delivering educational content, newsletters, and announcements (with your consent)
  • Administering surveys and soliciting feedback

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Quizera does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Educational Institutions

When you access Quizera through a school, college, or university, we share relevant academic data (grades, progress reports, engagement metrics) with authorized institutional personnel, including educators, administrators, and academic advisors, in accordance with institutional data sharing agreements.

4.2 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party service providers to support our operations, including:

  • Cloud infrastructure and hosting services
  • Payment processing and billing services
  • Email delivery and communication platforms
  • Analytics and performance monitoring tools
  • Customer support and helpdesk solutions

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal processes, court orders, or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Investigate potential violations of our policies or applicable laws
  • Protect against imminent harm to the rights, property, or safety of Quizera, our users, or the public

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity. We will notify you of any such change and provide options regarding your data.

Data Protection Commitment

All third parties with whom we share data are required to maintain appropriate security measures and comply with applicable privacy laws. We conduct regular audits of our service providers to ensure ongoing compliance.

5

Data Security

Quizera employs industry-standard technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction:

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher protocols. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption standards.
  • Access Controls: We implement role-based access controls, multi-factor authentication for administrative accounts, and the principle of least privilege to limit data access to authorized personnel only.
  • Infrastructure Security: Our systems are hosted on secure, SOC 2 Type II certified cloud infrastructure with redundant backups, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments, vulnerability scans, and penetration testing to identify and remediate potential weaknesses.
  • Employee Training: All personnel with access to personal data receive mandatory privacy and security training and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Incident Response: We maintain a documented incident response plan to promptly address any data breaches or security incidents.
Your Responsibility

While we implement robust security measures, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. Never share your password with others, and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. We will provide this information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format within 30 days of your request.

6.2 Correction and Update

You may update or correct inaccurate personal information through your account settings or by contacting our support team. We will promptly make the requested changes unless we are required to retain the original data for legal or compliance purposes.

6.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions. We will honor deletion requests unless we are legally required to retain the data or it is necessary for:

  • Completing transactions or providing requested services
  • Detecting and preventing security incidents or fraudulent activity
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Exercising free speech or ensuring the right of another user to exercise free speech

6.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You may opt out of receiving promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing message or adjusting your communication preferences in your account settings. Note that you cannot opt out of essential service-related communications.

6.5 Do Not Track Signals

Our platform currently does not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals. You may control cookies through your browser settings or third-party tools.

6.6 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a request through our Privacy Request Portal or contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@quizera.edu. We will verify your identity before processing requests and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

7

Children's Privacy

Quizera is committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws protecting minors' privacy.

7.1 Use by Minors Under 13

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13, except when used in an educational setting with verifiable parental or school consent. When a school provides consent on behalf of students under 13, the school assumes responsibility for complying with COPPA requirements.

7.2 Educational Institution Consent

When educational institutions authorize student use of Quizera, they represent that they have obtained necessary parental consent or are acting as the parent's agent under COPPA. Institutions must provide parents with notice of our data practices and obtain consent where required by law.

7.3 Limited Data Collection

For users under 13, we collect only the minimum information necessary to provide educational services, including:

  • Student first name and last initial (or anonymous student ID)
  • Class or grade level
  • Quiz responses and academic performance data
  • School-provided email address (if applicable)

7.4 Parental Rights

Parents of children under 13 have the right to:

  • Review their child's personal information
  • Request deletion of their child's information
  • Refuse further collection or use of their child's information
  • Receive notification of our information practices

Parents may exercise these rights by contacting their child's school or our support team at privacy@quizera.edu.

Enhanced Protections for Students

We do not use student data for targeted advertising, build personal profiles for non-educational purposes, or sell student information to third parties. All student data is used solely for educational purposes as authorized by the school.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Quizera uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage patterns, and maintain platform functionality.

8.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality, including authentication, security, and session management. These cannot be disabled without affecting site performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, settings, and previous actions to provide a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform, identify popular features, and detect technical issues.
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor platform speed, responsiveness, and overall performance to optimize user experience.

8.2 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that set cookies to collect usage data. These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

8.3 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may impair platform functionality. For more information, visit your browser's help documentation.

8.4 Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use web beacons, pixel tags, and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our Services and to deliver relevant content and advertisements.

Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with or link to third-party services, applications, and websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to these external services, which have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

9.1 Educational Technology Integrations

We may integrate with Learning Management Systems (LMS), student information systems, and other educational technology platforms to enhance functionality. When you use these integrations, data may be shared according to the terms of your institution's agreements with those providers.

9.2 Social Media and Authentication Services

If you choose to connect your Quizera account with social media platforms or use third-party authentication services, we may receive limited profile information as authorized by you and permitted by those platforms' privacy settings.

9.3 Third-Party Responsibility

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external services you access through our platform.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

10.1 Account Data

Active account information is retained for the duration of your account's existence. Following account deletion, we may retain certain data for up to 90 days to allow for account recovery, after which it is permanently deleted.

10.2 Educational Records

Academic data, quiz results, and educational content may be retained longer to support institutional requirements, academic transcript services, and compliance with educational record-keeping obligations.

10.3 Legal and Compliance Data

We may retain certain information longer when required by law, regulation, or litigation holds. This includes data necessary for tax, audit, and regulatory compliance purposes.

10.4 Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We may retain aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data indefinitely for research, analytics, and platform improvement purposes, as this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

International Data Transfers

Quizera operates globally and may transfer personal information to countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

11.1 Transfer Mechanisms

When transferring personal data internationally, we use approved transfer mechanisms such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by relevant data protection authorities
  • Adequacy decisions recognizing equivalent levels of data protection
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct where applicable

11.2 Data Processing Locations

Your data may be processed in the United States, European Union, and other countries where we or our service providers maintain facilities. We maintain the same level of protection regardless of processing location.

11.3 Regional Compliance

We comply with applicable regional privacy laws, including the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other jurisdiction-specific requirements.
