What is Quizera?

Quizera is a comprehensive e-learning platform designed specifically for Computer Science students. We offer interactive quizzes, study materials, learning games, and collaborative tools to enhance your educational experience. Our platform combines modern technology with proven teaching methods to make learning engaging and effective.

How do I enroll in a subject?

As a student, you can browse available subjects from your dashboard or the Browse Subjects page. Click on a subject to view its details, then click the "Enroll" button. Once enrolled, you'll have access to all topics, quizzes, and materials for that subject. You can track your progress and complete topics at your own pace.

How do I take a quiz?

Navigate to your enrolled subjects, select a quiz, and click "Take Quiz". You'll have a set time limit to complete all questions. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and enough time before starting. Your progress is automatically saved, and you can view your results immediately after submission. You can also review your answers in the quiz results section.

Can I retake quizzes?

Quiz retake policies depend on the settings configured by your teacher. Some quizzes allow unlimited attempts, while others may have a maximum attempt limit. Check the quiz details before starting to see the retake policy. All your attempts are saved, and you can review them in your quiz history.

How do I create a subject as a teacher?

From your teacher dashboard, click "Create Subject". Fill in the subject name, description, and other details. Once created, you can add topics, upload study materials (including PDFs), and create quizzes. Students can then enroll in your subject and access all your content.You can track student progress and engagement through the subject dashboard.

What types of questions can I create?

Quizera supports multiple question types including Multiple Choice, True/False, Identification, and Enumeration questions. Each type can be customized with points, time limits, and difficulty levels. You can also add images and formatting to make questions more engaging. Mix different question types to create comprehensive assessments.

How do notifications work?

Quizera sends real-time notifications for important events like new quiz publications, enrollment confirmations, quiz results, and announcements. You can access all notifications from the bell icon in the navigation bar. Notifications remain accessible even after logout and can be marked as read or deleted as needed.

How do I use the chat feature?

The chat feature allows direct messaging between students and teachers. Access it from your dashboard. Search for a user, click on their name, and start a conversation. All messages are persisted in our database, so you can access your chat history even after logging out. Chat supports typing indicators and read receipts for better communication.

What learning games are available?

Quizera offers several educational games including Fruit Catch (reaction time), Typing Speed Test (keyboard skills), and Code Debugger (programming logic). These games are designed to make learning fun while developing important skills. Access them from the Games section in your dashboard.

How can I track my progress?

Students can track their progress through the dashboard, which shows completed topics, quiz scores, subject progress percentages, and learning streaks. Each subject displays a progress bar showing how many topics you've completed. Your profile also shows overall statistics and recent achievements.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard security practices including password hashing, secure database connections, and data encryption. Your personal information is never shared with third parties. We comply with data protection regulations and regularly update our security measures. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Can I customize my profile?

Yes! Visit your profile page to customize your avatar (choose from various animals), update your bio, change your password, and manage account settings. Your profile is visible to other users when they search for you, helping create a connected learning community.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link. The link is valid for 1 hour. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. Make sure to use a strong password with at least 6 characters.

What should I do if I encounter a bug?

Please report any bugs or technical issues to our support team at support@quizera.com. Include details about what you were doing when the error occurred, your browser type, and if possible, a screenshot. We appreciate your feedback and work quickly to resolve issues.

Can I access Quizera on mobile devices?

Yes! Quizera is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Simply access our website through your mobile browser. We're also working on dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android to enhance the mobile experience.
